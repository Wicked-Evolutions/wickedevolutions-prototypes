Helena Willow — First Encounter Brief

Helena Willow is a spiritual guide, author, and course creator based in Gothenburg, Sweden. Her work bridges ancient wisdom traditions with contemporary therapeutic practices, creating spaces where people can reconnect with their authentic selves.

Voice & Tone

Warm, grounded, and direct. Helena writes as someone who has walked the path and now holds the lantern for others. Her voice carries authority without being authoritarian — she invites rather than instructs.

Core Themes

  • Spiritual development as a practical discipline, not abstract philosophy
  • The body as a gateway to spiritual awareness
  • Integration of shadow work and light work
  • Community and connection in the healing journey

Audience

Women aged 35–55 who have achieved external success but feel an inner call to deeper meaning. They are often at a crossroads — career transition, relationship shift, health challenge — and are looking for guidance that honors both their intelligence and their intuition.

What She Offers

  • 1-on-1 spiritual guidance sessions (online and in-person)
  • Group courses on seasonal spiritual practices
  • Written guides and blog posts on her website
  • Community gatherings in Gothenburg

Brand Positioning

Helena occupies a space between traditional therapy and new-age spirituality. She's more grounded than most spiritual coaches — her background in psychology gives her work clinical rigor — but more open to mystery than most therapists. This positioning is her strength and should be protected.
