The vision

Building in public.

"The Open Voice Platform isn't just a tech stack — it's a movement toward platform independence."

Wicked Evolutions is where builders share what they're creating, teach what they've learned, and evolve their practice together. From solo founders cutting SaaS costs to agencies deploying AI-powered WordPress at scale — this is where the tools come alive and the community grows.

What's inside

Learn. Build. Go deeper.

Three ways to engage, at whatever level fits your journey.

Free

Free Courses

Learn to set up and run the full stack. From zero to AI-powered WordPress. Installation guides, configuration walkthroughs, first workflow tutorials. Everything you need to go from "I've heard of MCP" to "my AI is managing my entire site."

Open

Build Journals

Document your journey. Show what you're creating. Inspire others building on the same platform. Real projects, real challenges, real solutions. The best way to learn is to build — and the best way to grow is to share what you've built.

Premium

Deep Dives

Premium workshops on advanced agent patterns, multi-site deployment, custom abilities development, and 1:1 implementation support. For builders who want to go further, faster — with direct access to the people who built the platform.

Community tracks

Find your people.

Three tracks, each with dedicated spaces, courses, and conversations. Join the one that fits — or all three.

Founders

Founders Track

Cut SaaS costs, own your platform, automate with AI. Focused on practical implementation for businesses — from first install to full automation. Replace $800/month in subscriptions with tools you control. Stop renting. Start owning.

Creators

Creators Track

Publish your vision at scale. For changemakers, educators, NGOs, and anyone with a message that matters. Focus on publishing pipelines, community building, and turning ideas into impact. Your words, your platform, your audience — no middleman.

Builders

Builders Track

For developers and agencies who want the full picture. Advanced agent patterns, custom abilities development, multi-site deployment strategies, and contributing to the open source ecosystem. Push the platform forward and shape what comes next.
