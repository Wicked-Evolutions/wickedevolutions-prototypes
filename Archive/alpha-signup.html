The Stack

Everything your AI needs
to run WordPress.

Two ability suites. Two transport bridges. One open protocol.

Abilities Suite for WordPress
The native WordPress layer. Content management, block parsing and editing, navigation menus, taxonomies, media, users, plugins, themes, comments, meta fields, settings, cache, cron, patterns, site health, REST discovery, rewrite rules. Your AI can read, write, publish, and manage everything WordPress natively supports — including designing with theme.json and building pages with blocks.
96 abilities · 17 modules · Open source · GPL
Abilities for Fluent Plugins
The business operations layer. FluentCRM, Community, Forms, Booking, Boards, Support, SMTP, Messaging, Auth, Snippets. Cross-module intelligence with user-360 views and engagement scoring. Conditional loading — only abilities for your active Fluent plugins register. Separate product, separate install.
158 abilities · 11 modules · Open source · GPL
MCP Adapter for WordPress
49 lines of PHP. Auto-discovers all registered abilities and serves them as MCP tools. The connector between WordPress and any AI client.
SSH Bridge + HTTP Bridge
SSH for maximum security (key auth, zero credentials). HTTP for remote teams (Application Passwords). Your choice of transport.
Works with Claude, Cursor, Windsurf, any MCP client
